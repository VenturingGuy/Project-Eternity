Gameplay

The World mod is a Turn-based strategy game with multiple players building and upgrading their base while capturing Zones to help their progression. Each Player has ressources they can use to construct Buildings or build new Units.

 

Zones

A Zone is defined by a group of tiles. When a Player has enough Units in a Zone, it will capture it if it not owned or if the current owner no longer have the minimum number of Units to control this Zone. Once captured, a Zone will allow its owner to construct Buildings on its tiles and will give ressources to its owner at the end of the turn.

 

Unit Actions

There are 3 types of Unit, normal Unit, harvest Unit or transport Unit. The normal Unit doesn't any special ability but will have higher stats than the other 2 types and is more suited for battle. The harvest Unit is the only type that can harvest ressources from ressources spots. The transport Unit can transport other Units, if it is destroyed while transporting Units, they will also be destroyed.

 

Buildings

Buildings of multiple sizes and uses can be constructed by using ressources. The more advanced a Building is, the more turns it will take to finish its construction. Some buildings are neutral all will be usable as long as there is a Unit inside it. There are 3 main types of Buildings, factories, support Buildings and defense Buildings.

A factory can use ressources to build new Units.

A support Building has a passive role only. Supports Buildings are: Radar, Teleporter, Research Center, Ressource harvester and Power Plant.

A defense building is only used to help in battle. Defense Buildings are: Turret, AA Turret, Wall, Mine and Gravity trap.

 

Factions

There are 3 factions, Robot ninjas, Trees and Insects and each has their own pros and cons. The stats of each factions are as follow (higher is better):

The Robot ninjas Units:

  • Damage: 5
  • Speed: 5
  • Defense: 2
  • Cost: 1
  • Build Time: 2

The Trees Units

  • Damage: 3
  • Speed: 1
  • Defense: 5
  • Cost: 5
  • Build Time: 1

The Insects Units

  • Damage: 1
  • Speed: 3
  • Defense: 1
  • Cost: 5
  • Build Time: 5

 

Robot Ninjas

  • Robots ninjas are the fastest and most powerful faction but their build cost is also the highest.

  • This faction's unique advantage are:

    • Ninja Catapult: A transport Unit that launches its content up to 10 tiles away.
    • Run On Water: Lights Unit can run on water without penalties but will die if they end their turn over a water tile.
    • Invisibility: Requires Research Center. A Player ability that makes a Unit invisible for 5 turns or until an attack is made.
  •  

Trees

  • Tress have the highest defense and are the cheapest to build but they are also the slowest and their build time is the highest.

  • This faction's unique advantage are:

    • Bird raid: A Player ability that sends a flock of birds. Can be used to damage an enemy Units inside any Zone you control or obstruct an empty tile in any Zone.
    • Seeds of War: When a Unit is attacked it will plant 1 seed in the surronding area that will change into a light Unit after 2 turns.
    • Exploding Acorn: Requires Research Center. Every heavy Unit unlocks an extra MAP Attack.

 

Insects

Tress have the best build cost and time but their defense and damage are the worst.

This faction's unique advantage are:

  • Swarm: A Player ability that sends a swarm of insects on every other Player not using the Insect faction, deplaying construction speed by 1 turn.
  • Healing Factor: Each turn an insect Unit will recover 20% of it's max HP.
  • Flight: Requires Research Center. Every light Unit unlock a flight ability.
